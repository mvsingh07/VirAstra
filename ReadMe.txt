VirAstra is an integrated web application designed to provide a holistic approach to well-being by combining physical fitness, self-realization, and nutrition planning in a single platform.

It is divided into three realms:

Isometric: Focuses on structured exercise routines to enhance physical strength and endurance.

Adhyatama: Encourages self-awareness and spiritual growth through guided meditation, mindfulness practices, and philosophical insights.

Sustenance: Offers personalized meal planning and nutritional guidance to support a balanced lifestyle.

VirAstra serves as a one-stop solution for individuals seeking to improve their overall well-being by harmonizing body, mind, and nutrition. Whether users are looking for workout plans, spiritual wisdom, or diet recommendations, IAS provides a seamless experience tailored to their needs.
